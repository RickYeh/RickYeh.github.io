Drivewayshare

Find or rent out parking spots around your city.

Drivewayshare is a proof of concept for an idea one of our teams had in creating an AirBnB for parking spots. Parking in a city can be tough, and at the same time, there are residents who may have unused parking spaces. So why not match the two? I jumped onto the team as a front-end developer and redesigned and enhanced many aspects of the site including the main search and results pages.

Tech Stack: AngularJS, Bootstrap, NodeJS, Express, Knex, Bookshelf, MySQL, Heroku
