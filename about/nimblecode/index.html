Nimblecode

Improve your code-typing speed competitively, either alone or with friends

Nimblecode is an app I created with a team of three others to create a fun, yet education game. It has a singleplayer mode, which pits players against ghost replays of the best recorded times, as well as a multiplayer mode, where players can race against their friends to see who types code the fastest!

Tech Stack: React, Redux, Bootstrap, NodeJS, Express, MySQL, Bookshelf, AWS(EC2), Mocha, Webpack
