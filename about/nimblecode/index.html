Nimblecode

Improve your code-typing speed competitively, either alone or with friends.

Nimblecode is a fun and educational coding game. In singleplayer mode, players compete against ghost replays of the best times. In multiplayer, players race against their friends to see who types code the fastest!

Tech Stack: React, Redux, Bootstrap, NodeJS, Express, MySQL, Bookshelf, AWS EC2, Mocha, Webpack
